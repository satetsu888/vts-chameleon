Colorize your model feely

With VTS-chameleon, you can change the colors of your models as you wish. You can group multiple ArtMeshes and set their colors.

Store favorite coordinates

You can save your favorite coordinates. You retrieve them whenever you want, and create new coordinates based on them.

Different colored uniforms, different skin tones, different eye colors, etc. There are many possibilities!
